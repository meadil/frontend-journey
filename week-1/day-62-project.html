About Me

I'm a cheerful sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea in Bikini Bottom.
I work as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, and I'm proud to say I'm the best fry cook in the ocean!
I love my pet snail Gary, jellyfishing with Patrick, and perfecting my Krabby Patty recipe.

My Skills

Fry Cooking

I can flip a Krabby Patty faster than anyone!
Mr. Krabs says I'm employee of the month... every month.
I know 1,001 ways to make a Krabby Patty, and I never mess up an order (well, almost never).

Jellyfishing

Patrick and I go jellyfishing almost every day at Jellyfish Fields.
I've caught jellies of all types and sizes.
My net technique is unmatched, and I even know how to communicate with them!

Bubble Blowing

It's all in the technique!
First, go like this. Then, spin around. Stop!
Double take three times. And here comes the best part... POOF!
A perfect bubble elephant, or boat, or whatever shape I'm feeling that day.

My Goals

My biggest dream is to get my boating license from Mrs. Puff's Boating School.
I've failed the test 1,258,056 times, but I'm not giving up!
I also want to keep making people happy with my Krabby Patties and maybe become manager of the Krusty Krab someday.
And of course, keep being the best friend Patrick could ever have!
