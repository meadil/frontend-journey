iPhone Air - The Thinnest iPhone Ever

Introducing the all-new iPhone Air, Apple's thinnest and lightest smartphone to date. At just 5.9mm thin and weighing only 140 grams, this device redefines what a premium smartphone can be.

Revolutionary Design

The iPhone Air features a stunning 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with a resolution of 2556 x 1179 pixels. Built with aerospace-grade aluminum and Ceramic Shield front cover, it's 4 times more resistant to drops than previous models.

Powered by the new A18 Pro chip built on 3-nanometer technology, the iPhone Air delivers incredible performance while maintaining all-day battery life. The chip features a 6-core CPU and 6-core GPU that makes everything from gaming to video editing buttery smooth.

Camera System

The advanced dual-camera system includes a 48-megapixel main camera and a 12-megapixel ultra-wide camera. Capture stunning photos in any lighting condition with Night mode, Deep Fusion, and Smart HDR 5. Record cinematic 4K video at 60 frames per second with Dolby Vision HDR.

The front-facing 12-megapixel TrueDepth camera supports Face ID and takes incredible selfies with Portrait mode.

Connectivity and Features

Stay connected with ultrafast 5G network support across all major carriers. The iPhone Air supports Wi-Fi 6E for blazing-fast wireless speeds and Bluetooth 5.3 for seamless connection to your accessories.

Available in five gorgeous colors: Midnight Black, Starlight Silver, Product Red, Ocean Blue, and Forest Green. Storage options include 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB.
